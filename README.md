# Prestige

### Part of #30DaysOfGraph Pilots

Project Prestige is an open, community-driven intelligence infrastructure designed to map how excellence emerges, compounds, and influences human progress across science, culture, technology, and institutions.

Modern AI systems can generate plausible answers, but they lack structured understanding of credibility, lineage, and influence. As a result, they hallucinate facts, overlook important relationships, and fail to reason about the context behind high-impact ideas.

Project Prestige addresses this limitation by building a Context Graph of Excellence — a structured knowledge system that models how ideas, individuals, institutions, and cultures interact to produce high-impact outcomes.

Instead of treating information as isolated facts, Prestige maps:

- intellectual ancestry
- institutional ecosystems
- interdisciplinary bridges
- diffusion of ideas across languages and cultures
- measurable signals of influence and recognition

The project is both:

a public learning platform for graph AI, embeddings, and reasoning systems
a foundational dataset for building verifiable AI systems

Prestige transforms fragmented knowledge into a navigable structure that allows machines and humans to reason about excellence with traceability and transparency.
